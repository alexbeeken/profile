About Me

I'm an aspiring full stack Junior Developer currently attending Epicodus in Portland. We've focused on Test Driven Development using MVC frameworks like Rails, Sinatra, Angular and Ember. I want to continue to learn the craft of writing code that is fast, makes sense and is easy to change. I love finding new ways of approaching problems, learning how to design better programs and learning how I can be a better member of a team. My plan is to work as a developer for at least the next few years, beefing up my math skills along the way. At some point I want to go back to school to get a masters in computer science or related tech field. I love thinking of interesting questions, hearing people's perspectives, working on passion projects and thinking of how I could change the world.

I have several interests outside of programming. I am a former choir teacher and classically trained singer. I also dabble in guitar and jazz piano. I have had a lot of musical adventures composing and singing with diverse groups. I hope to continue this hobby for the rest of my life!
